python-hawkey Tutorial¶

Setup¶

First of, make sure hawkey is installed on your system, this should work from your terminal:

>>> import hawkey
>>>

The Sack object¶

Sack is an abstraction for a collection of packages. Sacks in hawkey are powerful toplevel objects hiding lots of functionality. You’ll want to create one:

>>> sack = hawkey.Sack()
>>>

Loading RPMDB¶

hawkey is a lib for listing, querying and resolving dependencies of packages from repositories. On most linux distributions you always have at least the system repo (in Fedora it is most often called “The RPM database”). To load it:

>>> sack.load_system_repo()
>>>
